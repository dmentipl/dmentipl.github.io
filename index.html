About

I'm a PhD student at the School of Physics and Astronomy at Monash University.

I work with Daniel Price and Christophe Pinte on hydrodynamical and radiative transfer modelling of protoplanetary discs, using Phantom and MCFOST.

Code

I am the main author of Plonk, a smoothed particle hydrodynamics analysis and visualization tool written in Python.

In addition, I contribute to both Phantom and MCFOST.

Other packages for working with Phantom include:

  • phantom-config for working with Phantom configuration files,

  • phantom-setup for generating initial conditions for Phantom simulations,

  • phantom-build to make it easier to compile Phantom.
